Rising sea levels

Global warming is causing an ever accelerating rate of sea level rise due to the melting of ice caps and glaciers. This is also resulting in waves of high tide flooding towards areas in the coasts, submerging beautiful islands such as Tuvalu.

Endangered Ecosystems

Not only does the melting of ice sheets mean loss of habitat for Arctic animals, the drastic change will also threaten many species of animals as it will make it very difficult to adapt and survive in the harsher climate.

Food shortages

The depletion of natural resources due to climate change also results in shortage of food supply, especially for the those who are unable to afford the inflated prices. Floods and heat waves will bring implications of diseases and other health issues upon mankind.
